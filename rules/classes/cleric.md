## Memorization Rules
- Pray to deity each morning after sleep; choose any spells from full class list.
- No spell book required; divine spells freely chosen from the list.
- Reversed forms: choose at cast time by speaking words/gestures backwards.
- Deity disfavour: lose spell access if acting against alignment/religion tenets.

## Turning the Undead
Roll 2d6; compare to table. On success, roll 2d6 again for total HD turned/destroyed.
- **–**: Fail. **Number**: Need that roll or higher. **T**: Auto-success (turn). **D**: Destroyed.
- At least 1 undead always affected on a success.
- Mixed groups: lowest HD affected first.
