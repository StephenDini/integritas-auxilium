## Thief Skills at Level 6
Roll d% (≤ listed value = success). Hear Noise: roll 1d6 (1–3 = success).

| Skill | Level 6 | Notes |
|-------|---------|-------|
| Climb Sheer Surfaces (CS) | 92% | Roll per 100' climbed; failure = fall at halfway point |
| Find/Remove Treasure Traps (TR) | 40% | Roll to find, then separate roll to remove; once per trap |
| Hear Noise (HN) | 1–3 | Quiet environment only; referee rolls secretly |
| Hide in Shadows (HS) | 36% | Must be motionless; cannot move or attack while hiding |
| Move Silently (MS) | 45% | Referee rolls secretly |
| Open Locks (OL) | 45% | Requires thieves' tools; one attempt per lock per level |
| Pick Pockets (PP) | 45% | –5% per level of target above 5th; roll >2× required = noticed |

**Referee rolls secretly** for Hear Noise, Hide in Shadows, and Move Silently.

## Backstab
- Must attack an **unaware opponent from behind**.
- **+4 bonus to hit**.
- **×2 damage** (level 1–4); ×2 at level 6. (Damage multiplier increases at higher levels.)
- Works with any weapon.

## Other Abilities
- **Read Languages** (level 4+): Read non-magical text in any language with 80% success.
- **Scroll Use** (level 10+): Cast arcane spells from scrolls; 10% chance of malfunction.

## After Reaching 9th Level
Can establish a **Thieves' Den**, attracting 2d6 1st-level apprentice thieves. A successful thief may found a Thieves' Guild.
