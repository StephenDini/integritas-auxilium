## Special Abilities
- **Full weapon and armour proficiency**: Use any weapon, any armour, shields.
- **Attacks per round**: 1 (standard). High-level fighters (optional rule) may gain multiple attacks: 2/round at 20th, 3/round at 25th, 4/round at 30th.
- **No spells or thief skills**.

## Stronghold
- At any level (with sufficient funds), a fighter may build a castle or stronghold and control surrounding lands.

## After Reaching 9th Level
- May be granted a noble title (Baron/Baroness). The controlled land is known as a Barony.

## Combat Notes at Level 6
- THAC0 17 [+2]: needs a 13 to hit AC 4, 11 to hit AC 6, 9 to hit AC 8.
- With STR 13–15 (+1 bonus): needs 12 to hit AC 4.
- With STR 16–17 (+2 bonus): needs 11 to hit AC 4.
- With STR 18 (+3 bonus): needs 10 to hit AC 4.
