## Gems

Roll d20 per gem:

| d20 | Value |
|-----|-------|
| 1–4 | 10 gp |
| 5–9 | 50 gp |
| 10–15 | 100 gp |
| 16–19 | 500 gp |
| 20 | 1,000 gp |

Multiple gems of the same value may be combined (e.g., five 50gp gems can be a single 250gp gem).

## Jewellery

Each piece: **3d6 × 100 gp** (higher for parties above 3rd level, at referee's discretion).

Rough treatment (crushing, fire, lightning) halves jewellery value.

## Selected Notable Magic Items

### Miscellaneous Items (roll 1d4 for table, then d%)

**Bag of Holding**: Objects up to 10'×5'×3', 10,000 coins weight; bag itself weighs 600 coins when full.

**Boots of Speed**: Move at 240'(80') for up to 12 hours; must rest 1 full day afterward.

**Boots of Levitation**: Unlimited vertical flight at 20'/round; push off surfaces to move laterally.

**Boots of Travelling and Leaping**: No fatigue from travel; jump 10' high and 30' horizontal.

**Bracers of Armour**: Grant AC 8–1d4 [11+1d4]; no benefit if worn with armour.

**Brooch of Shielding**: Absorbs up to 101 hp of magic missile damage; then dissolves.

**Broom of Flying**: Flies at 240'(80'); two riders = 180'(60').

**Candle of Invocation**: Burns 4 hours total. Divine casters of matching alignment: +2 effective levels for memorization/casting. Others: +1 to attacks/saves.

**Cloak of Defence**: +1/+2/+3 bonus to AC and saves (roll 1d8: 1–4 gives +1, 5–6 gives +2, 7–8 gives +3). No effect worn over chainmail or heavier, or with magic armour.

**Cloak of Flight**: Transforms into bat wings; fly at 360'(120') for 2 turns, or 180'(60') for 4 turns, or 120'(40') for 8 turns. Rest required afterward.

**Crystal Ball (arcane casters)**: View any scene 3/day (1 turn duration). Variants: +Clairaudience (hear as well), +ESP (read thoughts of one creature viewed).

**Displacer Cloak**: Attacker's hits suffer –2 penalty. +2 to saves.

**Dust of Appearance**: Sprinkled to reveal invisible/displaced creatures in 10' radius.

**Dust of Disappearance**: Sprinkled to grant complete invisibility (all in 10' area).

**Elven Cloak and Boots**: 90% undetected when moving; near-silent movement.

**Figurine of Wondrous Power**: Various animal figurines that animate on command.

**Flying Carpet**: Carries 1–3 people; speed depends on load.

**Gauntlets of Ogre Power**: STR 18 for carrying/attack purposes; +3 to hit and damage.

**Portable Hole**: 10' deep cylindrical hole in a cloth patch; folds up to pocket-size.

**Ring of Protection**: +1/+2/+3 to AC and saves.

**Ring of Invisibility**: Turn invisible at will; broken by attacking or casting.

**Rope of Climbing**: 60' rope; climbs on command, ties/unties knots, holds up to 10,000 coins.

**Wand of Fire Balls**: Fires fireball (6d6 each, save for half). Charges: 2d10.

**Wand of Lightning Bolts**: Fires lightning bolt (6d6, save for half). Charges: 2d10.

**Wand of Magic Detection**: Detects magic in 20' radius. Charges: 2d10.

**Wand of Paralysis**: Cone of paralysis (save vs wands or paralysed). Charges: 2d10.

## XP from Treasure

1 gp = 1 XP. Treasure is the primary source of XP in OSE. Magic items have no fixed sale value for XP purposes.

**Party at level 6 — XP needed to reach level 7:**
- Thief: 24,001 (needs 48,000 total)
- Wizard: 40,001 (needs 80,000 total)
- Fighter: 32,001 (needs 64,000 total)
- Elf: 64,001 (needs 120,000 total)
- Cleric: 25,001 (needs 50,000 total)
