# Saving Throws Reference

## The Five Categories
| Abbreviation | Category | When Used |
|---|---|---|
| D | Death / Poison | Targeted by death ray or exposed to poison |
| W | Wands | Targeted by effect from a magical wand |
| P | Paralysis / Petrification | Effect that paralyses or turns to stone |
| B | Breath Attacks | Dragon breath or other monster breath weapon |
| S | Spells / Rods / Staves | Baneful spell, magical rod, or staff effect |

## Rolling a Saving Throw
- Roll 1d20; result must be **≥ the listed value** to succeed.
- **Damaging effects**: success = half damage.
- **Non-damaging effects**: success = effect entirely avoided.
- **Poison**: failure = usually death (damage not halved on success).
- **WIS modifier** applies to all saves except breath attacks.
