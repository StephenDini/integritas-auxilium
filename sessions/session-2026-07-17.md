## Encounter Log

### Gargoyle-style Animate Statues (Office / early room)
Two winged beast statues on pedestals animated and attacked (a repeat of the office-room setup). Killed. (Rooms this tier are numbered erratically by the DM — 62, 64, 65, 66, 67, 68, 70, 72, 74, 77, 78, 80, etc. — many were empty kitchens/mess/guard rooms.)

### Were-Tigers ("Siegfried & Roy") — Room 64
A man in a glittery bodysuit with a white tiger; the two **swap forms** (a were-tiger act). Non-hostile lotus-eaters, awaiting a chance to perform in the big city again. OJ tried hard to buy/recruit/ride them; they declined. OJ traded **wine** for a **gem**. **600 XP** (roleplay).

### Three Minotaurs — Room 72
Behind a barred door (bull silhouette). Party scouted via drilled peephole + crystal ball, got a **surprise round**. Bruce's **Lightning Bolt** (chained: 20/10/10, then a second bolt for 22) killed all three; **Hold Person failed** (minotaurs are monstrosities, not humanoid). **825 XP** (275 each). Loot: **4 gems worth 1,700 gp**.

### Hell Hounds (Copper-Pile Room, one level down via trap door)
A circular room of "natural death" paintings with a huge pile of **~9,000–10,000 copper pieces** between two ladders, guarded by fire-breathing **hell hounds**. OJ mistook them for friendly (like his blink dog) and tried feeding them (beef jerky, the scorpion corpse, wine, the lion's-head trophy) — they breathed fire (14 dmg to Burt, etc.). Killed with Rowan's flaming sword, arrows, lightning, and magic missile. (Some NPC looters — "three guys on bicycles" — fled with sacks of copper.)

### Wooden Statue-Creatures
Small (3-ft) man-like animate wooden statues. Bruce cast **Web** (no save) to immobilize them, and the party destroyed them with ranged attacks (spears, daggers, mace, magic missile) as they hung helpless. A **trap door** in the floor (spotted by the wood elf) led deeper.

### Two Medusas
**Petrification** threat. **Burt was turned to stone** (he wore a minotaur head to look through its mouth-hole but still failed his save). The party fought looking at the floor / blind (–4 to hit) and killed both. **350 XP** (175 each). Burt was restored with Dougal's **Stone to Flesh** scroll. Loot: **11 gems (~1,100 gp)**, a second **rope of climbing**, a **potion of heroism**, an arcane scroll, and a **ring of strength (+1 STR)**.

### Three Giant Weasels
Killed (Bruce lightning bolt 26, etc.). A dead Syncytian victim carried **7 gp and 2 arcane scrolls**.

### Two Mummies — Room 74
Mummy fear aura: **save vs paralysis or be paralyzed with fear**. **Dougal rolled a 1 and was paralyzed with fear** (the cleric, terrified of mummies). The table explicitly discussed whether a **paralysis antidote** could cure the fear-paralysis — DM ruled **no**. Killed with fire (Rowan's flaming sword, etc.). **800 XP**. Loot: **4 pieces of jewelry (~3,200 gp)** and **5,000 gp**.

### Displacer Beasts — Room 77 (Blink Dog Death)
Panther-like creatures with tentacles; the blink dog reacted badly ("apoplectic"). Bruce fired the **Wand of Wonder**: the delusion roll (77) made him think he cast one thing, but the actual effect was a **Fireball** (save vs wands; 10 dmg on save, 20 on fail). It killed the displacer beasts — **and OJ's blink dog**, which was at 1 HP, took 20, and was disintegrated ("crystified," nothing left). **1,000 XP** (displacer beasts) + a grim **125 XP** for the dog's death. The dog's remains were bagged in case of future raising.

### Two Trolls — Room 80
8-ft green regenerating humanoids tossing grain sacks. **Rowan ("Troll Slayer")** shone here — his flaming sword is **+2 to hit and +1d4 fire damage vs fire-sensitive creatures**, tailor-made for trolls. Both killed and **burned** so they couldn't regenerate. **1,300 XP** (650 each). Loot: one troll's sack held **6,000 gp**.

## Discoveries / Rules Notes

- **Wand of Wonder mechanic (confirmed):** roll d100 and spend a charge; the **first roll is a delusion** (what the wielder *believes* happens), the **second roll is the real effect**. This use produced a real Fireball (which killed the blink dog). Held by **Bruce**; ~98 charges when acquired, so it is being drawn down. See `items/wand-of-wonder.md`.
- **Paralysis antidote vs fear-paralysis:** DM ruled a paralysis antidote does **not** cure fear-induced paralysis (mummy fear aura). Fear is a mental effect, not poison.
- **Hold Person** does nothing to non-humanoid "monstrosities" (minotaurs).
- **Medusa petrification:** save vs Petrification, no ability-score modifier; looking away / at the feet lets you fight at **–4 to hit**; Stone to Flesh reverses it.
- **Trolls:** regenerate; must be **burned** (or acid) to stay dead. Rowan's flaming sword is especially effective (+2 hit, +1d4 fire).
- **Crystal ball** (Bruce): scry a room's contents — visual 3×/day, visual + audio 1×/day; higher success in familiar areas. Used to pre-scout rooms this session.
