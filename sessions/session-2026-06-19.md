## Demetrius / Darius (Plot Reveal — Corrects Prior Open Thread)

**Last session's "charmed/cursed white robe" was a possession, not a curse.** The robe carried the spirit of **Demetrius**, who possessed Bruce (explaining the impatience, the "hurry / don't waste time" behavior, and Remove Curse having no effect).

Demetrius's account, delivered through Bruce after the fight:
- His father was an ardent follower of **Usamgaras** who spoke out against Apocryphon and one night never returned (presumed killed).
- His brother **Darius** was ambitious and converted to Apocryphon, causing a rift.
- Apocryphon's priests held a rigged "lottery" selecting sacrifices; Demetrius's **mother was selected and taken by the priests** despite Demetrius begging Darius to intervene.
- Darius **killed Demetrius** before he could take revenge. Demetrius has haunted these tunnels for years, waiting for a "worthy vessel."
- With Darius now dead, Demetrius declared his intent to see **Apocryphon killed** — noting that killing the god would sever the Apocryphon clerics from their power — then his spirit departed. Bruce retained all memories of the possession.

## Discoveries / Rules Notes

- **Black pudding:** dissolves non-magical weapons/gear; cutting or smashing splits it into multiple puddings; **fire** is its weakness (recoils, shrinks, dries out).
- **Wraiths:** incorporeal undead; a hit **drains an experience level**. Distinct from wights (which have corporeal bodies). Higher-level undead carry severe drain effects.
- **Gargoyles:** "wet"/stone-flesh — stone-cure antidotes and turn-undead do nothing.
- **Giant desert scorpion:** heavy carapace deflects most weapon hits until worn down.
- **Cleric spell-swap:** this DM lets clerics burn any prepared spell of an appropriate level in place of Cure Light/Serious Wounds (a house rule to encourage varied prepared spells).
