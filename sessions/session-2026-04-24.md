## Discoveries This Session

### The Ziggurat Vocal Tube System

The structure is a **ziggurat**. The three hollow statue-pillars in the secret room are vocal tubes that run up through the pyramid to the apex. At the apex are multiple speaking tubes. These tubes project outward to the nearby city.

The system was used to **impersonate a god** — someone speaking into the tubes would have their voice amplified and broadcast across the city, giving the appearance of a divine voice.

**Conclusion:** The gods heard in this region were a ruse. No actual divine presence — a constructed deception using the ziggurat's tube system.

**Triggered by:** Creepy child — examining or interacting with the child led the party to discover the apex tube system.
