## Discoveries This Session

### The Ziggurat Vocal Tube System

The structure is a **ziggurat**. The three statue-pillars in the secret room are hollow tubes running up through the ziggurat to a **god box** at the apex. The god box receives the tubes from below and projects vocal tubes outward to the nearby city.

The system was used to **impersonate a god** — someone speaking into the tubes below would have their voice carried up to the god box and broadcast across the city.

**Conclusion:** The gods heard in this region were a ruse. No actual divine presence — a constructed deception. The god box is the mechanism.

**Triggered by:** Creepy child — examining or interacting with the child led the party to discover the apex tube system.
