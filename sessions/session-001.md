# Session 001

**Date:** 1/12/2025
**Notes by:** Matt

## Overview

The party convened in Lockleed, each having their own reason to seek the Codex of Infinite Wisdom. They were recruited by Alton (USAF Ranger) on behalf of the sage Nemmerle, learned the nature of the Codex and the quest to find the four Noldori who carry its translation glyphs, received the ARTEMIS Moongate disc, and traveled northeast to Castleton — where they were hired by the fence Linus Larabee to clear a cliffside manor of monsters.

## Events

### Lockleed — Nemmerle's Manor

The party met Alton at their inn. He wears a cloak bearing an eagle on a circle with three stars and the motto *"integritas auxilium optimum."* He led them to Nemmerle's grand but visibly declining manor.

Each party member was permitted to consult the Codex of Infinite Wisdom. The text was unreadable — appearing as indecipherable script to all of them.

Nemmerle explained the situation: a Noldori master had divided the Codex's translation scroll into four glyphs, tattooing one on each of four Noldori students, then sent them to the four cardinal directions by the Four Winds. The Keep was subsequently destroyed in battle with a warlord; both the warlord and the master disappeared. The Codex survived.

Without the four glyphs tattooed on the four Noldori, the Codex cannot be read.

Alton presented the party with the Moongate: a platinum disc marked *PROJECT A.R.T.E.M.I.S., (TM) North-Central Positronics, STL* — a pre-Reckoning artifact. It has six red gem teleport anchors around the perimeter and pearl and onyx gems at the center.

The party buried one moonstone in Lockleed to serve as a return portal anchor.

They were instructed to travel to Threshold and find Commander Swan.

### Travel — Lockleed to Castleton

The party traveled approximately one week northeast to reach Castleton.

### Castleton — The Cloak & Stagger

At the bar, the party bought Skull Splitters for three dwarves:
- Ironpants (Nicholas)
- Dragonbailt (Tavin)
- Happy (Kim Hammerhand)

They also met **Linus Larabee** — a human fence who goes by "Double L, Give em Hell." Linus knows things. He asked the party to clear monsters from a cliffside manor he had "legally" purchased. The party agreed.

### The Cliffside Manor

**Exterior / Approach:** The manor sits at the top of a large hill. Sheer unscalable walls flank both sides; the rear backs against a cliff face dropping hundreds of feet to rocks and a river below. The only viable entry is the front. The party briefly discussed alternatives, then OJ, Rowan, and Dougal walked up and asked the guards to leave — knowing they wouldn't. Gnolls and a half-orc were stationed at the entrance. The party fought their way in.

**Guard Turrets and Sleeping Quarters:** Explored, cleared. Dougal was attacked by a fire beetle hiding under a bed. He kept two fire sacks from the creature.

**Secret Passages:** Fraxiga located secret passages throughout the manor.

**The Fountain Room:** Burt spotted a mirror mounted on the ceiling above a fountain and shot it down. This disabled a Water Weird trap. He grabbed a goblet from the area.

**The Half-Orc with the Deed:** The party found a half-orc in possession of a deed to the manor and a badly forged duplicate. Both were retrieved for Linus.

**Locks:** Burt picked his first lock of the campaign. He also obtained a brass key and a large key from the manor.

**The Dead Thief — Frank and the Eight Skeletons:** The party discovered a group of eight skeletons in the dungeon. The skeletons each had LVNV poker chips placed in their eye sockets — pre-Reckoning artifacts from Las Vegas, Nevada (old world). The tabard with two red handprints shaped like a bird was associated with this group, indicating they belonged to the same organization or faction. One skeleton — Frank — was half-pulled into a hole in the ground. Frank also had a merchant's scale and a sack of 20 gems on him. The party tied a rope around Frank's remains and hauled the skeleton free. Dougal and OJ then investigated the hole by tossing a fire sack down it — the sack fell far enough that it was barely visible and barely audible on impact, indicating a deep cave system below. Burt now carries Frank and wears the tabard.

**The Lift:** The party discovered a lift descending to dungeon levels and caves beneath the manor. These were not fully explored this session.

**Burt picked the first lock of the campaign** during the manor exploration.
