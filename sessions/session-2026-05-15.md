## Discoveries / Rules Notes

### Shadows are NOT Undead (OSE)

Confirmed in-play: shadows cannot be turned by clerics. They are intelligent, incorporeal monsters but **not undead**. Only magic damages them.

Dog (blink dog) cannot harm shadows — natural attacks are not magical.

### Cure Disease destroys Green Slime

Confirmed previously (Session ~10-11).

### Stone Shape on Sarcophagus

Effective tactic: seal a sarcophagus lid via Stone Shape, then open a small inspection hole. Used on King Alexander's coffin to safely access loot after banshee fight.

### Charm vs Curse

Remove Curse does not break Charm. Folklore: physical pain (e.g., breaking a finger) may snap a charmed person out of it. Theory applied to Bruce's white robe situation but not tested.

### Silence vs Banshee

Silence (cleric spell) effectively nullifies a banshee's wail attack. 15 ft radius, 2 rounds per cleric level (12 rounds at level 6). Side effect: also silences party spellcasters in radius.
