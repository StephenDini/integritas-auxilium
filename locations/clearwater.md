# Clearwater

## Type
Town on the westward desert route toward Las Vegas (past the Pitching Post)

## Description

A town the party passed through on the journey to Las Vegas (a pre-archive session; surfaced in the DM's recaps). Home of **Roderick and Magdalene**, a "weird, creepy" brother and sister.

## Known Info

- Roderick and Magdalene clarified that "**the deceiver**" (the hint the party got from the gypsy ghost at the Pitching Post) was **not** the necromancer in the tower, but someone their **grandfather** used to correspond/trade with out of the **city of Las Vegas**.
- This pointed the party onward to Las Vegas (on the way to the tower).
