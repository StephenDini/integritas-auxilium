# The Tower

## Type
Unreached destination west of / beyond Las Vegas

## Description

A tower flagged repeatedly on the westward journey (from the DM's recaps). It houses a **necromancer** — believed to be the **source of the undead** roaming the region.

## Known Info

- The gypsy ghost at the Pitching Post pointed the party to "the deceiver in the tower."
- In Clearwater, Roderick and Magdalene clarified the **deceiver** is a separate person (a Las Vegas contact), but the **necromancer in the tower** is real and undead-connected.
- Las Vegas lies "on the way to the tower," so the party detoured into the ziggurat/underground city first.

## Status
Unreached. An open westward objective. See [[pitching-post]], [[clearwater]].
