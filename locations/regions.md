# Regions of Newhon

## Overview

The main continent is Newhon. It is divided into four broad quadrants. Large towns are believed to be built over the ruins of Ancient (pre-Reckoning) cities. Old Tek artifacts surface throughout.

---

## Northeast — The Five Kingdoms

Dangerous territory. Cold and cruel. Giants are a known threat. Political structure implies multiple kingdoms, likely in conflict or tension.

---

## Southeast — Kingdom of the Rat

King Walter's domain. Swampy, dangerous terrain. The Ratters who live here have a distinctive greeting: they say "Be Seeing You" and make an "okay" symbol over one eye.

See: locations/kingdom-of-the-rat.md

---

## Southwest — The Nemmex Desert

Harsh wasteland. Glowing water is poisonous. The dead walk. Known landmarks:
- **Temple of the Wanderer**
- **City of the Phoenix**

Valen was mapping forest on the edge of this region when he found the spider amulet.

---

## Northwest — The Verdant Lands / Threshold Region

Relatively easy terrain. Verdant farmlands, fresh water. Goblinoid raids are a persistent threat. Home to Threshold.

**Route to Threshold:** Follow the Shipley River south to the Misery River, then northwest toward the Mountains of Lost Ore. Threshold sits at the feet of those mountains on the near side. The far side is The Big Gulp.

---

## Key Geography

### Shipley River
Runs through or near Lockleed. Traveling south along it leads to the Misery River junction.

### Misery River
Connects from the Shipley River. Traveling northwest along it leads toward the Mountains of Lost Ore and Threshold.

### Mountains of Lost Ore
Mountain range in the northwest. Threshold is located at their feet on the eastern/southern side.

### The Big Gulp
Located on the far (western/northern) side of the Mountains of Lost Ore. Nature and contents unknown.
