# The Pitching Post

## Type
Magnet-rail way station on the desert route west (pre-Reckoning North Central Positronics build)

## Description

A desert way station on the magnet-rail line the party rode toward Las Vegas (pre-archive travel, from the DM's recaps). Like other way stations, it is a sealed North Central Positronics structure openable only with the **Moongate**.

## Known Info

- The party met an **old gypsy woman** here who turned out to be a **ghost**; they **fixed her curse**.
- In return she gave the hint: "**seek the deceiver in the tower**." (Later clarified in Clearwater — the deceiver is a Las Vegas contact, not the tower's necromancer.)
