# Castleton

## Overview

A settlement northeast of Lockleed, approximately one week's travel. The party passed through here early in the campaign and was hired by Linus Larabee to clear a cliffside manor.

## Key Locations

### The Cloak & Stagger

A bar. Barkeep is Billy. Known as a gathering spot for adventurers and dwarves. The party bought Skull Splitters for three dwarves here and met Linus Larabee.

### The Cliffside Manor

A large manor on the cliffs above Castleton, purchased by Linus Larabee (legality disputed). The party cleared it of gnolls and half-orcs in Session 1.

**Manor layout (explored):**
- Exterior/entrance: guarded by gnolls and half-orcs
- Guard turrets
- Sleeping quarters (fire beetle encountered under a bed)
- Fountain room (Water Weird trap, disabled by destroying ceiling mirror)
- Secret passages (found by Fraxiga)
- Room with half-orc holding deed + forged deed
- Room where dead thief skeleton (Frank) was found half-pulled into a hole in the ground
- Lift leading to dungeon/cave levels below (not yet explored)
