# Croaking Fane

## Overview

A swamp temple complex. The entrance is built into or through the open mouth of a massive stone frog statue, covered in moss. Ancient carved stonework visible in the background. Located in or adjacent to swampland.

## Known Info

- This is the site of module 2 in the campaign (The Croaking Fane, DCC). The party has already cleared it.
