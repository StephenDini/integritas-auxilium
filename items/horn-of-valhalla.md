# Horn of Valhalla

## Owner
Party item (blown by "Nick"'s character; carried by the party — confirm which PC on sheets). Jokingly called the "horn of Gondor" at the table.

## Type
Summoning horn

## Effect (this DM's version)

When blown, roll twice: once for **what** is summoned, once for **how many**. The summoned warriors fight for the party and then **vanish ("back to Valhalla") when the combat ends** — they do not linger.

## Notable Uses

- **Session ~10–11 (2026-04-24):** blown during the burial-tomb fight; summoned **7 second-level fighter "berserkers"** (roll: type + d6+3 = 7). They brawled the 25 skeletons, cut down ~10, then vanished when the fight was won. See [[../sessions/session-2026-04-24]].
