# World History

## The Creation

In the beginning there were four: Father Chronos, Mother Terra, Sister Luna, and Brother Sol. From them came all life on the world of Newhon.

The first people were One People. They practiced One Magic, which they called Tek. With it they built cities to the sky. They forgot the gods entirely.

## The Reckoning (Barron's Folly)

The One People's worship of Tek led them to catastrophe. Ancient worshippers of Tek accidentally summoned a demon or titan known as ATLAS. ATLAS caused the destruction of Luna — Sister Luna was shattered, her rubble left orbiting the world.

The organization known as **North Central Positronics** built ARTEMIS in response — a silver disc that now hangs in the night sky, visible among Luna's rubble. With their work complete, the survivors fled through the **Tannhauser Gate**.

Without leadership or structure, chaos erupted. This period is known as **Barron's Folly** — when the world burned.

Only ARTEMIS remained in the sky, a silver disc surrounded by Luna's shattered remnants.

## The Splintering

The Three (Chronos, Terra, and Sol — Luna being destroyed) eventually forgave humanity and allowed life to return. But the age of unity was over. They splintered everything that had been whole:

- **One People** became: Men, Elves, Dwarves, Gnomes, Halflings, Orcs, Goblins, Lizardmen, Giants
- **One Magic** split into the Eight Schools of Arcane Magic
- New gods emerged to fill the void

## The True Nature of the World

**The setting is post-apocalyptic Earth.** Newhon is the current name for what was once America. The Reckoning destroyed modern civilization and the world that preceded it. What the current peoples call "ancient history" and "Old Tek" is simply the technology and culture of the pre-collapse world — our world.

Pre-Reckoning place names survive as artifacts and fragments:
- **LVNV** = Las Vegas, Nevada — a major city of the old world. Items marked LVNV are pre-Reckoning artifacts originating from that location.
- **USAF** = United States Air Force — the USAF Warriors are an order descended from or styled after this institution.
- **North Central Positronics** — a pre-Reckoning organization/corporation.

This context is largely lost to the current peoples of Newhon. Most treat Old Tek as magic or legend.

## The Current Age

The main continent is **Newhon** (formerly America). Large towns are rumored to be built over the ruins of Ancient cities. Pre-Reckoning artifacts ("Old Tek") survive in scattered locations — powerful, poorly understood, and dangerous.

The name "Walter" is traditionally applied to the ruler of the Kingdom of the Rat regardless of the actual ruler's name — a lineage custom or erasure of identity. The current Walter's real name may be Jeffrey.

## ARTEMIS

PROJECT A.R.T.E.M.I.S. was created by North Central Positronics (STL) before the Reckoning. The Moongate disc is one of its artifacts — a teleportation device using gem anchors. The silver disc visible in the night sky among Luna's rubble is ARTEMIS itself.

## The USAF Warriors

A cult or order called the **USAF Warriors** seeks ancient knowledge — specifically Old Tek artifacts and information about the pre-Reckoning world. Their insignia is an eagle on a circle with three stars. Their motto: *"integritas auxilium optimum."* They maintain rangers in the field to locate and recover artifacts and information.

## Taelendra's Account (Corroborating Source)

The Noldori elf Taelendra ("Tinker Belle"), held captive by King Walter, corroborates the historical account with additional detail: ancient worshippers of Tek summoned ATLAS, who destroyed Luna. North Central Positronics built ARTEMIS and fled through the Tannhauser Gate. Without them, chaos erupted — Barron's Folly burned the world.

She has direct knowledge of: Moongates, Old Tek magic, North Central Positronics, New Atlantis, ARTEMIS, and the Tannhauser Gate — suggesting access to pre-Reckoning records or firsthand proximity to surviving knowledge.
