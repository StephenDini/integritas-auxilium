# Demetrius

## Role
Vengeful ghost; former Syncydian; briefly possessed Bruce

## Location / Affiliation
Haunted the tunnels/levels beneath the Las Vegas ziggurat for years. Son of a follower of Usamgaras. Brother of Darius.

## Description
The spirit of a Syncydian man killed years ago by his own brother, Darius. Manifested by possessing a wearer of a white hooded robe recovered on the burial tomb level. While possessing Bruce (session ~13), he made him impatient, pushy, and obsessed with not wasting time; Remove Curse had no effect because it was possession, not a curse.

## Known Info

- His father was an ardent follower of **Usamgaras** who spoke out against Apocryphon and one night never returned (presumed killed).
- His brother **Darius** was ambitious, converted to Apocryphon, and caused a rift in the family.
- Apocryphon's priests ran a rigged "lottery" for sacrifices; Demetrius's **mother was selected and taken**. Demetrius begged Darius to intervene; Darius did not.
- Darius **killed Demetrius** before he could take revenge. Demetrius then haunted the tunnels for years, waiting for a "worthy vessel."
- Possessing Bruce, he guided the party through the level (knowing its traps) to Darius's counting-room and had Darius killed by Fireball.
- His parting counsel: **killing Apocryphon would sever its clerics from their power.**

## Status
Departed. His spirit left Bruce once his revenge against Darius was complete. Bruce retained memory of the possession. See [[darius]].
