# Polycrates (the Herald)

## Role
Herald / spokesman of a group of yellow-robed worshippers beneath the Las Vegas ziggurat

## Location / Affiliation
The area toward the city archives (north), beneath the ziggurat. One of six humans in **bright yellow robes and human-face masks**.

## Description

Introduced himself as "Polycrates, the herald." He and his fellow worshippers **mistook Dougal for the resurrected King Alexander** (and the party for royalty), bowing and offering service (session ~10–11).

## Known Info

- Believes Dougal is **King Alexander**, the old ruler of the city. Dougal leaned into the impersonation (used the Staff of Healing as a "kingly touch").
- The worshippers **escorted the party north toward the city archives**, warning them to zigzag around snakes on the floor.
- Reverent and non-hostile; sang praises and saw the party off ("fare thee well, my lord").

## Status
Alive. Represents ongoing **social leverage**: this group treats Dougal as their king. See [[../sessions/session-2026-04-24]].
