# Darius

## Role
Priest of Apocryphon; money-changer / temple treasurer (deceased)

## Location / Affiliation
Counting-room (Room 58) on the cleric's-study level beneath the Las Vegas ziggurat. Follower of Apocryphon. Brother of Demetrius.

## Description
A man in clear vestments, flanked by six armed guards, tallying coin and skimming 10% "for the temple" when the party found him. Recognized the possessed Bruce ("Killed you once...") and ordered his guards to attack.

## Known Info

- Ambitious; converted to **Apocryphon** during its rise, splitting from his family.
- Behind the sacrifice of his and Demetrius's **mother** (taken by Apocryphon's priests) and implicated in their **father's disappearance**.
- **Killed his brother Demetrius** years ago before Demetrius could act against him.

## Status
Dead. Killed by Bruce's Fireball (as Demetrius) along with his six guards; Dougal's Silence kept the room's bystanders unaware. His ashes yielded **plate mail +2**, a **shield +2**, and a **mace +2**. See [[demetrius]].
