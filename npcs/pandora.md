# Pandora

## Role
Leader of the Ariadne faction (Las Vegas underground city)

## Location / Affiliation
The Ariadne faction area beneath the Las Vegas ziggurat, reached via the faction-button hallway. Follower of Ariadne (the maiden/mother/crone — the Fates).

## Description

Met seated at a table with two women guards, all in **bronze chainmail over green tunics** and **bronze masks of Ariadne's face** (the statue atop the pyramid). Encountered when the party emerged from the secret door of the Ariadne treasury (session ~10–11).

## Known Info

- She and her guards were surprised — and unhappy — that the party came out of their **treasury's** hidden door (the party had just looted it: 1,000 gp, a 600 gp gem, 300 gp in jewelry).
- Told the party they were **trespassing in sacred areas** and to leave; declined to help beyond ejecting them (she pushed the hallway button to send them out).
- The Ariadne followers are nonviolent and keep to themselves.

## Status
Alive; left on wary terms. An unexplored potential relationship. See [[../locations]] and the Ariadne faction notes in the README.
