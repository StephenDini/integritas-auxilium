# Commander Swan (Voronwë / Taelendra)

## Role
One of the four Noldori glyph-bearers; captive captain under King Walter; former commander of the Silverwood Sentinels

## Location / Affiliation
Aboard King Walter's ship (Kingdom of the Rat). Formerly: Threshold / Silverwood Sentinels.

## Description
Noldori elf. Silver hair. Known publicly as "Commander Swan" or "Voronwë." Her true name is Taelendra.

She is under a curse that has lasted at least 80 years: her feet cannot touch dry land. She is also cursed to be unable to speak or write her own name or the names of others — which is why she goes by "Swan."

King Walter calls her "Tinker Belle."

## Known Info

**The Curse and its origins:**
Approximately 80 years ago, following an event called "the Siege," Taelendra's master teleported her away via a spell that deposited her in saltwater. She found a derelict vessel run aground and boarded it for shelter. King Walter (the ruler of the era) made a deal with her: she would captain his deep-sea fishing ship in exchange for food, water, and necessities.

She has rerouted the ship's magic engines — described in terms suggesting pre-Reckoning technology: repulsors, ley threads, magnets/magic rocks, rat-powered wheels.

She sabotages Walter's Old Tek finds whenever she can. She wants to see her brother and friends and escape Walter's control.

**Knowledge:**
She has extensive knowledge of pre-Reckoning technology and history:
- Moongates (how they work)
- Old Tek magic generally
- North Central Positronics
- New Atlantis
- ARTEMIS
- Tannhauser Gate

She can read maps and open locks.

**The glyph:**
She is one of the four Noldori bearing a translation glyph tattooed by the Noldori master. Her glyph is required to read the Codex of Infinite Wisdom.

**Threshold:**
She founded or renamed the settlement of Threshold (formerly Whitman). On the name: *"Threshold was originally Whitman. I wanted to change that, make it bigger than a person. People can come and go, but ideas; ideas persist. This is the threshold of civilization. This is where law stands before chaos. Good before evil. Civility before savagery."*

She leads or led the Silverwood Sentinels and sent a letter to Linus Larabee thanking him for sending the party to clear the Whispering Caves, proposing a waterway trade route between the Silverwood and Castleton areas.

The party extended gratitude to the Silverwood Elves after clearing the Whispering Caves; Swan spoke to them and offered the Silverwood Elves' ongoing support.

**North Central Positronics gem (Rowan's sword):**
Taelendra noted that Rowan's sword contains a North Central Positronics gem. Context: frog idol and snake idol are referenced in connection with these gems. The gems have tiny swords that fit into sheathes on their items — incorrect orientation ruins the magic.

## Status
Captive. Aboard King Walter's ship. Walter has offered to release her if the party clears Lizard Men from his swamp.
