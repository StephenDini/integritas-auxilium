# Fox-Masked Siblings

## Role
Rival/neutral treasure hunters (a brother and sister)

## Location / Affiliation
Met in the **main chapel of Sin City** beneath the Las Vegas ziggurat (session ~13), refilling waterskins at the fountains. Not aligned with the party.

## Description

Two **red-haired** humans — a brother and sister — in small **brown fox masks**. Non-hostile treasure hunters "from the upper parts," working the same ruins as the party.

## Known Info

- Had come up from the underground city (Sin City), where they'd resupplied on mushrooms and food.
- Told the party the passage **north** of the chapel leads to the **main entry chamber into Sin City**.
- Knew nothing about what lay **below** (the zoo caverns / Apocryphon).
- The party offered to transcribe its map of the three cleared levels above; the two sides parted peacefully (the party debated killing them but did not).

## Status
Alive; parted on neutral terms. Names unknown. See [[../sessions/session-2026-06-19]].
